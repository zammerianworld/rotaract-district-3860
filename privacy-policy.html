Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Rotaract District 3860 ("the District," "we," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information this website collects, how it's used, and the rights you have under the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) of the Philippines. This site is run by District volunteers to share information about Rotaract clubs, events, and leadership in District 3860 - it is not a commercial service, and we collect the minimum information needed to do that.

What We Collect

We only collect personal information that you choose to give us, such as when you:

  • Send an inquiry through a "Contact for Details" email link or the Contact section
  • Submit a club or district report through a linked Google Form
  • Reach out to a club or officer via a listed email or Facebook link

This may include your name, email address, club affiliation, and the contents of your message. We do not run our own account-registration or login system, and we do not knowingly collect information from children without a parent or guardian's involvement.

How We Use It

Information you share is used only for the purpose you shared it for - for example, responding to your inquiry, following up on a club report, or coordinating an event. We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information, and we do not use it for advertising.

Third-Party Services on This Site

To keep this site lightweight and free to run, we rely on a few third-party services that may process data on our behalf. Each operates under its own privacy policy:

  • Google Drive / Google Forms - hosts club photos and collects report submissions
  • Google Calendar - powers the embedded event calendar
  • Facebook - powers the embedded council and district Facebook feeds; Facebook may set its own cookies when these load
  • Vercel - our hosting provider, which may log standard technical data (e.g. IP address, browser type) for security and performance

We don't control how these third parties process data beyond what we submit to them, and we encourage you to review their respective privacy policies if you have concerns.

Data Retention

We keep personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purpose it was collected for - for example, until an inquiry or report has been addressed - and we do not retain it indefinitely.

Your Rights Under the Data Privacy Act

As a data subject under RA 10173, you have the right to:

  • Be informed that your personal data will be, is being, or was processed
  • Access a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or outdated information
  • Object to the processing of your data
  • Erasure or blocking of your data under certain conditions
  • Data portability, where technically feasible
  • File a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (NPC) if you believe your rights have been violated

To exercise any of these rights, reach out through the Contact section of this site. For complaints the District cannot resolve, you may also contact the National Privacy Commission directly.

Security

We take reasonable organizational and technical measures to protect the information you share with us. That said, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we can't guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time as the site evolves. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy or how your data is handled? Reach out via the Contact section of this site.
